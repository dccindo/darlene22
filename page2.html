This book is about Nikki J. Maxwell's adventure while planning her 16th birthday party. Along the way Nikki and her 2 bestfriends (Zoey and Chloe) face challenges like budgets, invitations accidentally being sent out, embarrasment and more. At the start Nikki was very excited for her birthday party, but the further they planned it kept seeming like the birthday party was going to turn into a disaster, but besides the cruddy disasters they faced there were also funny moments like when Nikki and her parents accidentally ate Brianna's (Nikki's little sister) cookies that turned out to be made out of dog biscuits!

After lots of planning, the 3 girls (Chloe, Zoey, and Nikki) decided that the birthday party theme was luau! Nikki ended up wearing a classic grass shirt with a floral top. Her cake matched her outfit! A lot of Nikki's friends and family members attended, even Nikki's archinemesis Mckenzie Hollister came to! They rented a large pool that soon was filled with her relatives! They took tons of pictures, and it wasnt only the Maxwell family who ate some of chef briannas delicous dog biscuits! Looks like both Mckenzie and Tiffany shared the pleasure with the family!
