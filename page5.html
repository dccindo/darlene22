Matilda

This book is about a young girl named Matilda Wormwood. Her father, mother, and brother are rather snobby, selfish and mean people. They hardly noticed matilda. As she grew up she learned how to complete her daily needs herself. After a few years, Matilda finally built the courage to ask her father Mr Wormwood, for something she desperately wanted, a book. Of course her father questioned her for her request, and denied. So Matilda decided to walk a few blocks to the library everyday. As this continued Matilda had turned 6-7 by now. Her parents were so focused on themselves they forgot about their own daughter's birthday. Not just one almost all her birthday's by now. Matilda found comfort from the characters in the books she read. After a long time, her parents finally let her go to school.

Matilda was happy that she got to experience school for the first time. Although her expectations of a nice school with nice teachers wasn't exactly a reality. The princapal Ms Trunchbull, was very unpleasant. She was mean to all of the students. It doesn't end there, Ms Trunchbull grabbed a girl by her pigtails and threw her over the playground fence! On the other side, there was Ms. Honey. Matilda's teacher. Ms Honey was a very kind teacher who acceted every kid in the school she met.After going through adoption papers Ms. Honey ended up being Matilda's principal, teacher, and mother!
